pomodoro?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. We will break down your work into intervals separated by short breaks. By default these work intervals are 25 minutes long and the breaks are 5 minutes long each. After 4 intervals, or pomodoros, the timer will stop. if you are not complete with your work, feel free to start up the timer again, however it is encouraged that you take a 15-30 minute break.

The point of this technique is that you solely focus on work during the pomodoros, so if you are distracted during a pomodoro, hit stop and start over! You can configure the intervals if you feel the interval lengths just aren't for you. Play around, and see what works for you.

Last but not least, this is a fully working web app, feel free to add it to your home screen!
